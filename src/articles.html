Flowers and their meanings

Flowers have been used for centuries as a way for people to share emotions they cannot express in words. This practice of using individual or arrangements of blossoms as a communication tool is called “the language of flowers” and has been practiced in many societies, both ancient and modern.

Top ranking flowers

Did you know?

Roses are the most commonly purchased flowers. Followed closely by carnations, which as we all know, can last two weeks in a vase, looking fresh the whole time. Chrysanthemums come in third, and of course, they have a long vase life.
