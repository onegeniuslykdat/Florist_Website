Welcome to Busybee Florist

Your One stop shop for all your floral needs

We are a small family-run local business, located and operating in Cairville for 5 years. We have, however, over 25 years of experience in floristry.

We offer the exceptional quality and choice of fresh flowers & foliage tailor-made to suit any occasion. We also do next-day home delivery (as long as the order is made by 3pm the day before).

In response to COVID 19, we do not have readymade flowers available for general walk-in trade. Please call or use our website to make your order.
